# Phishing Simulation Template

## **Target:** SecureSense Employees

**Subject:** URGENT: Mandatory Admin Password Expiration - Action Required

**From:** IT Helpdesk (not-real-it-helpdesk-securesense@outlook.com)

**To:** Employee

**Date:** [Current Date]

---

**Message Body:**

Dear User,

Our security monitoring systems indicate that your SecureSense admin password expires in exactly **2 hours**. As part of our mandatory security policy, you must update your credentials immediately to avoid account lockouts.

Please click the secure link below to reset your password now:

[https://securesense-admin-portal-reset.net/reset](http://fake-link.com)

If you fail to do so, your administrative privileges will be suspended, and you will need to contact the HR department to regain access.

Thank you,

**SecureSense IT Support**
_Keeping your data safe._

---

## **Red Flags to Identify:**
1. **Urgent Tone:** The email creates a false sense of urgency ("expires in exactly 2 hours", "action required").
2. **Generic Greeting:** Addresses the employee as "Dear User" instead of their actual name.
3. **Mismatched Sender Domain:** The email originates from a suspicious or personal-looking address (`not-real-it-helpdesk-securesense@outlook.com`).
4. **Deceptive Links:** The displayed text shows a plausible domain, but hovering over it reveals a different underlying URL (`fake-link.com`).

## **Reporting Protocol:**
If an employee encounters an email resembling this script, they should not click any links or download attachments. Instead, they must forward the email to the designated incident response team at `security-incident@securesense.internal` and mark it as potentially malicious in their email client.
